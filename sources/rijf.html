Welcome to our 22nd Edition

Jazz in June is back! Don’t miss 9 days of legendary performers, familiar favorites, and new discoveries from around the globe in one of the world’s leading and largest jazz festivals. Our festival is presented in several series or types of shows: Ticketed Headliners, Free Show Headliners, Free Shows, Club Pass Series, Jam Sessions, and Jazz Workshop. You can browse the lineup below by date, artist, venue, and series, and plan your schedule!
